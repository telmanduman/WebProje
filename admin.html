Suleyman Demirel University is a private higher educational institution located in Kaskelen, near Almaty.

Nowadays the university has 4 faculties:

  • Faculty of Law and Social sciences;
  • Faculty of Education and Humanities;
  • Faculty of Engineering and Natural Sciences;
  • SDU Business school.

SPT INTERNATIONAL is an internal Olympiad for foreign applicants at SDU University. The main purpose of the Olympiad is to assist candidates in selecting a future profession and providing them with grants and discounts at various levels. At the Olympaid, each pupil can win an SDU internal grant or discount by showing his / her knowledge in the chosen direction.
